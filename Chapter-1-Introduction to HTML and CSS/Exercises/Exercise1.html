French Toast Recipe

The French toast recipe can be used with different types of bread like whole wheat bread,brown bread,cinnamon raisin,Italian or French. It is deliciously served hot with maple syrup.

INGREDIENTS

  • WHITE BREAD
  • EGGS
  • MILK
  • SUGAR
  • SALT
  • BUTTER

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk,eggs,sugar,and a pinch of salt in a shallow bowl
  3. Lightly apply the butter on medium-heated pan.
  4. Dunk the white bread in the mixture,soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer the dunked bread to the medium-heated pan and fry each side 3-4 minutes
  6. Serve hot.
